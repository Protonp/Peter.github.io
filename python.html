Groundrunner unit 3

In the groundrunner I created a background and a coin. A then created a guy who would go around and collect this coin. when the man touched the coin the coin would go to a random spot on the map and the score would inscrease. Displayed at the top of the screen was a timer that counted down and the score. When the man collected 7 points the game would say "Winner." If the man ran out of time the game would say "GameOver."
