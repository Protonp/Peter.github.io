My Favorite Movie

Meet Dave

Summary

Meet Dave is a comedy about small aliens who live inside a spaceship that looks like a human. The spaceship,Dave, is played by Eddy Murphy, who is also on of my favorite comedians. Dave goes through many awkward encounters as he tries to accomplish his quest of draining all the salt from the ocean. Meet Dave Extended Summary

The Hotdog Scene

In this scene Dave gets in a hotdog eating contest in order to impress a girl. He dumps hotdog after hotdog into his mouth at an alarming rate.

Why I like Meet Dave

Although old Meet Dave combines comedy and scifi in an amazing way. It portrays the super smart aliens as a confused people in an unknown world. The movie also goes out of its way to make you laugh as the aliens try to figure out the mysteries of Earth
